About

I grew up in Lake Tahoe, studied 'Business Management Economics' at UC Santa Cruz, and moved to New York in 2003 to work on Wall Street (where I worked for 7 years). In 2010, I decided I wanted a new challenge and moved back west. I chose Web Development.

After learning the ropes of Web Development, I started a business that revolves around a hobby I've had since I was a little kid - collecting things. It's called 'The Sneaker Savant' and it's been a wild ride. If you get a chance, head on over and take a look.
